3. There are few specialized doctors at the hospital. There are not all types of medical equipment.

Suphanburi
Provincial Administrative Organization
1. There is a very clear change in the structure of age groups, the proportion of the young population (0-14 years) has decreased, while the proportion of the elderly population (60 years and over) has increased.
2. Personnel problems such as there are few civil servant personnel. Personnel are not performing their duties in the correct position, etc.
3. The work is not yet fully integrated by all parties.
4. Policy changes causing continuous work, plan new operations and performance indicators are not clear.
5. Budgets are being decrease and government subsidies are not a specified criteria.

Pain points :
Nonthaburi Provincial Administrative Organization
1. The budget of local government organizations is limited.
2. The increase in the elderly group. This will affect the living conditions and quality of life of the people in Nonthaburi Province. The proportion of elderly people (aged 60 years and over) increases every year, while the proportion of working age people and children decreases.
3. Problems with overcrowding in nursing units, such as the number of beds being insufficient for the number of patients, etc.
4. The budget is high, but the demand for the budget is also very high.

5. Self-funded and sponsored budgets are still not enough to solve the problems of the people.
6. Income collected by the Provincial Administrative Organization itself is small. Compared to income from government subsidies The revenue received from the government is uncertain.
7. Revenue collection still does not receive as much cooperation from the private sector as it should.
