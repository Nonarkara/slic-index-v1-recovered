Located at 1082 Ratchadamnoen Rd., Khlang, Mueang, Nakhon Si Thammarat, 
80000 Thailand, Nakhon Si Thammarat City Municipality has demonstrated exceptional 
innovation and dedication in addressing the critical issue of flooding within their municipal 
area. Their project named "Innovative Flood Control using a Smart City Mobile Application in 
Nakhon Si Thammarat City Municipality, Thailand," has garnered recognition and acc laim on 
both national and international levels. 
In particular, I would like to highlight that Nakhon Si Thammarat City

exemplary efforts in leveraging digital technology to safeguard the lives and properties of its 
residents while also significantly reducing the financial burden associated with flood 
management. 
Furthermore, it i s with great pride that we share news of the recent 
achievement of the Smart City Mobile Application, which received the Best Partnership 
Award in 2023 alongside DEPA at the esteemed World Smart City Expo 2023, hosted by the

serves as a testament to the fruitful collaboration between Nakhon Si Thammarat City 
Municipality and DEPA in spearheading innovative solutions for urban resilience.
